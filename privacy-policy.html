We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

8. Location Permissions

Android

Our Android apps request the following location permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — Precise GPS location for safety tracking
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — Approximate location as fallback
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION — Background location for continuous safety monitoring (optional, only for Parent and Student apps)

iOS

Our iOS apps request the following location usage descriptions:

  • NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription — Location access while the app is in use
  • NSLocationAlwaysAndWhenInUseUsageDescription — Location access in the background (optional)

You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.
